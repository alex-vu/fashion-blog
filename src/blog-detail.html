    Not only physical health but also "mental health". A place that fosters organic communication that is not just a company food.

    The impact of health on personal productivity is already widely known. Efforts to maintain health are essential for high performance business people. However, the “health” aspect that companies reflect in the system is often limited to physical health only.

    Not only in terms of physical health, but also in terms of mentality, the dining team at CRAZY CO. , LTD. With health at the top of management priorities, we devise daily meals and communications for that purpose. Wishing the health of our employees, team leader Sawako Kimura said, the role of a dining team that is not just corporate food.

    Structure to make person who can be healthy by oneself

    -Please tell us about the functions of the dining team.

    The dining team prepares meals and snacks for employees daily in the kitchen at the office. It is a so-called company food of welfare program. All ingredients used are delivered by the dining team, choosing only the ones that you trust to touch and touch.

    The goal is not just to eat, but to "get more people who can be healthy on their own". To that end, we are conscious of “a place that creates organic communication” that supports mental health.

    -Organic communication?

    I agree. In addition, talking with the producer made me more aware of the “beginning of life” and “connection”. We are usually in the city and forget that we are in the city of concrete, but we live in harmony with rich nature and animals. There is soil, and there are plants growing well there, and the trees have a very rich harvest. When it comes to the sea, there are many types of fish swimming.

    People tend to think of being at the top of a pyramid-shaped ecosystem, but we are in the flow of life that starts from the earth and the sea. I became more aware of such “the beginning of life” and “connection”.

    We, the dining team, receive life from producers who grow and distribute plants and animals, prepare them to eat more deliciously, and deliver them to employees. So you are the one who connects the baton of life. I do not want to forget that, and I would like to convey it properly.

    To team supporting exercise, sleep, human relations

    ― Please tell us about your future prospects and goals.

    The immediate goal is to deepen the motivation and awareness of individual employee health as a dining team. For this purpose, we are currently considering an initiative to bring home-made side dishes.

    For example, suppose that there is a person who thinks "I should eat cup ramen properly at night". If you have one CRAZY dining side dish, you may be thinking of preparing only the rice and miso soup yourself.

    From that point on, I would like to be able to support the concept of “health” more broadly and to be a team that can fully support exercise, sleep, and human relationships to ensure psychological safety.

    However, the basis for the dining team to keep in mind is what I think or "prayer". We strongly sympathize with the ideal that our employees are trying to give, so we can not release the "health" that we tend to lose in the process. It is impossible to get in the body of all the employees and cure the bad place, or to put a hand to cure it, but it is not good to put "prayer" in the dish saying "I hope you get well by eating this". Because you can.

    Of course, I think that productivity as a business is important. However, I think that I would like to continue to deliver a moment when I feel "delicious" honestly, and a rich lunch with friends who can share that feeling.
